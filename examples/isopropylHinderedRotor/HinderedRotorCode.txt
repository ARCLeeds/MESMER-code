      <bondArray>
        <bond id="b1" atomRefs2="a1 a2" order="1"/>

      <!--No explicit main DOSCMethod. Default is inserted-->

        <me:periodicity>3</me:periodicity>
      </me:ExtraDOSCMethod>

        <me:periodicity>3</me:periodicity>
      </me:ExtraDOSCMethod>

The method name in <me:ExtraDOSCMethod> is read in
gDensityOfStates::calcDensityOfStates().
The other elements are optionally read in HinderedRotorA.
Some of them may not be necessary.
